Community Involvement

Local School Reading Programs

Book Haven proudly sponsors reading programs at five local elementary schools, providing books and organizing author visits to foster a love of reading from an early age.

Monthly Book Drives

Every month, we organize book drives to collect gently used books for community centers, shelters, and libraries in underserved areas.

Literacy Volunteering

Our staff actively participates in local literacy programs, offering tutoring services to help improve reading skills across all age groups.
